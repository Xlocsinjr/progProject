Introduction
This page aims to visualise the world's greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions from 1970 to 2012 and how these emissions relate to a country's GDP.

Emissions world map

The world map shows the GHG emissions of countries.

  • Hover over a country to see its GHG emission in kt CO2 equivalent.
  • Click on a country to add the country to the scatter plot and the bar chart.

    GHG emissions and GDP

    The scatter plot shows the GHG emissions and GDP of the countries selected in the world map.

  • Hover over a dot to see the exact GHG emission and GDP.
  • Tick the checkbox to change the y-axis from GHG emission to GHG emission per GDP.

    GHG emission per commercial sector

    The grouped bar chart shows the GHG emissions per commercial sector of the countries selected in the world map.

    NOTE: the GHG emission for the forestry sector should be read as a negative number, as greenhouse gases are removed from the atmosphare by forestry.

  • Hover over a bar to see the exact GHG emission.

    Chart options

  • Click on the dropdown button to reveal a menu to remove countries from the scatter plot and the bar chart. Click on the buttons with a country name to remove that country from the scatter plot and the bar chart.
  • Tick the checkbox next to "Show GHG emission per GDP" to change the y axis of the scatter plot to GHG emissions per GDP in Kg CO2 equivalent per USD.
  • Tick the checkboxes of the commercial sectors to select which commercial sectors will be shown in the bar chart.
